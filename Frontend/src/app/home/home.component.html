About Us

City Hospital is a state-of-the-art healthcare facility dedicated to providing the best medical services to our patients. Our team of experienced doctors and staff are here to ensure your well-being.

Established in 1990, City Hospital has grown to become a leading healthcare provider in the region, known for our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced medical professionals, and patient-centered approach.

Our mission is to deliver high-quality healthcare services that meet the needs of our diverse community. We believe in treating each patient with respect, dignity, and empathy, ensuring that they receive the best possible care. Our team of highly skilled doctors, nurses, and support staff work tirelessly to create a safe and comfortable environment for our patients and their families.

At City Hospital, we offer a wide range of medical services, including:

  • Emergency Care
  • Outpatient Services
  • Inpatient Services
  • Surgical Services
  • Maternity and Childcare
  • Cardiology
  • Orthopedics
  • Neurology
  • Oncology
  • Diagnostic Imaging
  • Laboratory Services
  • Rehabilitation Services

Our hospital is equipped with the latest medical technology and equipment, enabling us to provide accurate diagnoses and effective treatments. We continuously invest in upgrading our facilities and training our staff to stay at the forefront of medical advancements.

We are proud to be a part of the community and are committed to making a positive impact on the lives of our patients. Our outreach programs and health education initiatives aim to promote wellness and prevent illness, ensuring a healthier future for all.

Thank you for choosing City Hospital as your trusted healthcare partner. We look forward to serving you and your family with excellence and compassion.

Our Services

At City Hospital, we are committed to providing comprehensive healthcare services to meet the diverse needs of our patients. Our state-of-the-art facilities and experienced medical professionals ensure that you receive the highest quality care. Below are some of the key services we offer:

  • Emergency Care

    Our Emergency Department is open 24/7, equipped with the latest technology and staffed by highly trained medical professionals. We provide immediate care for life-threatening conditions, injuries, and illnesses, ensuring that you receive prompt and effective treatment when you need it most.

  • General Surgery

    Our General Surgery department offers a wide range of surgical procedures, from routine operations to complex surgeries. Our skilled surgeons use advanced techniques and minimally invasive methods to ensure the best outcomes and faster recovery times for our patients.

  • Pediatrics

    Our Pediatrics department is dedicated to the health and well-being of children from infancy through adolescence. Our compassionate pediatricians provide comprehensive care, including routine check-ups, vaccinations, and treatment for illnesses and injuries, ensuring your child's healthy development.

  • Cardiology

    Our Cardiology department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of heart conditions. We offer a range of services, including cardiac consultations, diagnostic testing, interventional procedures, and rehabilitation programs, all aimed at promoting heart health and preventing cardiovascular diseases.

  • Pharmacy

    Our on-site Pharmacy provides convenient access to prescription medications and over-the-counter products. Our knowledgeable pharmacists are available to answer your questions, provide medication counseling, and ensure that you receive the correct medications for your needs.

  • Orthopedics

    Our Orthopedics department offers comprehensive care for musculoskeletal conditions, including fractures, joint disorders, and sports injuries. Our orthopedic specialists use advanced diagnostic tools and treatment options, including surgical and non-surgical methods, to help you regain mobility and function.

  • Neurology

    Our Neurology department provides expert care for neurological disorders, including stroke, epilepsy, multiple sclerosis, and Parkinson's disease. Our neurologists use cutting-edge technology and personalized treatment plans to manage and treat these complex conditions.

  • Oncology

    Our Oncology department offers comprehensive cancer care, including early detection, diagnosis, and treatment. Our multidisciplinary team of oncologists, surgeons, and support staff work together to provide personalized care and support throughout your cancer journey.

  • Diagnostic Imaging

    Our Diagnostic Imaging department is equipped with the latest imaging technology, including MRI, CT scans, X-rays, and ultrasound. Our radiologists provide accurate and timely diagnoses to support your treatment plan and ensure the best possible outcomes.

  • Laboratory Services

    Our Laboratory Services offer a wide range of diagnostic tests, including blood work, pathology, and microbiology. Our state-of-the-art lab ensures accurate and timely results to aid in the diagnosis and treatment of various medical conditions.

  • Rehabilitation Services

    Our Rehabilitation Services include physical therapy, occupational therapy, and speech therapy. Our experienced therapists work with you to develop personalized treatment plans to help you recover from injuries, surgeries, and medical conditions, and improve your overall quality of life.
